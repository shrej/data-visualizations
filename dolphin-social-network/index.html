Visualizing Dolphin Social Network

The graph below depicts an undirected social network of frequent associations between 62 dolphins in a community living in Doubtful Sound, New Zealand.
D. Lusseau, K. Schneider, O. J. Boisseau, P. Haase, E. Slooten, and S. M. Dawson, Behavioral Ecology and Sociobiology 54, 396-405 (2003)
Data provided by the University of California, Irvine - Network Data Repository
