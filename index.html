Explanation:

This graph represents the deviation in temprature from the 1951 – 1980 mean in 0.01 degrees, for each quarter between 1880 to 2015. Each line represents a deviation from the base period, and is color coded.
A look at the graph shows a clear increase in temprature over the years.
The data represented here is a subset of data obtained from NASA Goddard Institute for Space Studies.

Axes

X Axis: Years
Y Axis: The deviation from the 1951 – 1980 mean in 0.01 degrees.

Interactivity

Hover on chart: See Values for that particular year.
Hover on quarter in legend: See Data for that particular quarter only
Click on quarter in legend: Enable/Disable lines for that quarter.
Scroll up/ down while on chart: Zoom in out a particular period.
